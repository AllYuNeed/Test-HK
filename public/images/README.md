## 🎨 Image Requirements

### Technical Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG (JPEG)
- **Resolution:** 
  - Hero images: 1920x1080px (16:9) or 1600x1600px (1:1)
  - Gallery images: 1200x1200px (1:1) or 1600x900px (16:9)
- **File Size:** Max 500KB per image
- **Quality:** 80-90% compression
- **Color Profile:** sRGB

### Visual Style:
- **Industrial aesthetic** - dark charcoal/graphite backgrounds
- **Professional photography** - clean, well-lit product shots
- **Consistent lighting** - soft shadows, depth
- **Premium feel** - high-quality rendering
- **Context shots** - machines in factory/industrial settings where appropriate

## 📸 Photography Guidelines

### Composition:
1. **Hero Images:** 
   - Front 3/4 view showing machine controls
   - Clean, uncluttered composition
   - Machine centered or slightly off-center
   - Professional industrial backdrop

2. **Gallery Images:**
   - Alternate angles (side, back, top, close-ups)
   - Detail shots of controls/features
   - Context shots in use (if applicable)
   - Different color variants (if available)

### Lighting:
- Soft, diffused studio lighting
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Highlight metallic surfaces
- Create depth with subtle gradients
- Maintain consistent mood across series

### Styling:
- Industrial professional aesthetic
- Dark charcoal/graphite backgrounds
- Steel gray and blue accents
- Clean, organized presentation
- Premium quality feel
